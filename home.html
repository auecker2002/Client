Wagner's Lanes and the Complexx

Welcome To Chippewa Valley’s #1 Rated Bowling Alley

Wagner’s Lanes is not your typical bowling alley. Equipped with 26 lanes, a full menu, ample space, and competitive party packages, Wagner’s is the perfect place for all occasions and all ages. Wagner’s also features The Complexx, an innovative sports and entertainment facility which is home to a full-service bar complete with rooftop seating, ample event space, three volleyball courts, bean-bags, and volley- pong. The opportunities in The Complexx are endless, easily making it the hottest spot in the Eau Claire area.
